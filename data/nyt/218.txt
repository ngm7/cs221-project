BOOK REVIEW

A bibliographic note last Sunday with a review of the Japanese story collection “The Lonesome Bodybuilder,” by Yukiko Motoya, omitted the translator’s name. She is Asa Yoneda.

ARTS & LEISURE

An article on Page 20 about the rapper Offset misstates the relationship between Offset’s mother, Latabia, and his father figure, Kevin. Kevin is Latabia’s ex-husband, not her husband.

Errors are corrected during the press run whenever possible, so some errors noted here may not have appeared in all editions.
