“Momentum Generation” explores surfing in the ’90s. And the Pittsburgh Symphony Orchestra performs in commemoration of the victims of the Tree of Life Congregation shooting.

What’s on TV

MOMENTUM GENERATION 10 p.m. on HBO; also streaming on HBO platforms. The filmmakers Jeff and Michael Zimbalist explore how surfing evolved from a novelty beach hobby into a cutthroat competitive sport by the 1990s, thanks in part to one talented group of teenagers that included Kelly Slater, Rob Machado and Shane Dorian, among others. Using footage from the surfers’ personal archive, this documentary looks at how members of this group raised the stakes in professional surfing, and how their friendships changed in the process.

JEFF BECK: STILL ON THE RUN 7:30 p.m. on Showtime; also streaming on Showtime platforms. Jeff Beck is often revered as one of the most influential rock guitarists, a musician who rose to fame with the Yardbirds and played with some of the most important performers in the music industry. In this documentary, Jimmy Page, Eric Clapton, Rod Stewart, Joe Perry and others who played alongside Beck discuss highlights from his prolific career and his artistic inventiveness.
