Each of the standout interiors featured in T this year came with an equally memorable back story. Lined with outsize Georgian buildings, Henrietta Street in Dublin has been, over the years, home to both tenement squalor and aristocratic grandeur. Here, the music room at 12 Henrietta Street retains its original floorboards from 1737 and plasterwork and finish from 1780. The mirror over the fireplace is mid-19th century.
