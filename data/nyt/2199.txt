Several brands presented clothes this season that were elegant, romantic — and yet decidedly laid-back. Take, for example, Marni, which showed cozy oversize coats covered in sequins, or Calvin Klein, where vibrant marabou tops were styled with more casual, slouchy pants. All this is a blessing for anyone facing down a slew of holiday parties this month. Here, ten ideas for glamorous — and yet relaxed — holiday dressing.

1. GLITTERY EYES | There’s no more straightforward, or more striking, way to bring life to an otherwise bare face than a dusting of glitter on the lids — and no shade feels more seasonally appropriate than gold.
