The 61st annual awards will be broadcast by CBS from the Staples Center in Los Angeles on Sunday, Feb. 10.

• Kendrick Lamar has the most nominations, with eight. Drake follows with seven.

• The folk singer-songwriter Brandi Carlile surprised with six nods for her album “By the Way, I Forgive You.”

• After years of tension over the awards’ struggles with diversity, the number of nominees in the four general fields expanded from five to eight.
