Pantelleria, Italy | The craggy, windswept Italian island of Pantelleria was immortalized in Luca Guadagnino’s 2016 movie “A Bigger Splash,” but it has long been a favorite hide-out for fashionable Italians, including Giorgio Armani and the actress Isabella Ferrari, who both have houses here. Dotted with whitewashed dammusi (dome-roofed stone dwellings) and ringed with hidden coves, the island perfectly embodies understated Mediterranean glamor.
