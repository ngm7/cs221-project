Health care was a top issue in voters’ minds for the midterm elections, and a Texas judge’s recent ruling that the Affordable Care Act is unconstitutional has added to the debate.

We’d like to hear about what parts of the system you think are broken, and what works well and could serve as models for reform. What actions are you hoping the new Congress will take? Feel free to tell us how your experiences have shaped your opinions.

Please send your responses — no more than 200 words — by Wednesday, Dec. 26, at 10 a.m., Eastern time. We plan to publish a sampling.

Include your name, city/state and contact information, and put “remedies” in the subject line.
