This Weekend I Have … a Half-Hour and a Naughty Streak

‘The Detour’

When to watch: Now, on Hulu.

Natalie Zea and Jason Jones star as the parents in what sort of seems like a family sitcom — but is actually a giddy, filthy and often surprising saga about being on the lam. Season 3 is now streaming, although you’re better off starting at the beginning, since there are a lot of surprise twists and changing aliases. Don’t be fooled by the presence of tween characters: The material can be pretty blue.
